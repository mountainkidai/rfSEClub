Now we ask the question that separates ordinary thinkers from elite engineers:

> How do we think without assumptions?

---

Most people think by analogy.

They ask:

```text
What has been done before?
```

and then slightly improve it.

---

First principles thinkers ask:

```text
What is undeniably true?
```

and build upward from reality itself.

---

Most people reason like this:

```text
Horse
↓
Faster Horse
```

First principles thinkers reason like this:

```text
Transportation
↓
Energy
↓
Motion
↓
Vehicle
↓
Car
```

---

Most people optimize existing systems.

First principles thinkers create new systems.

---

The purpose of this phase is to learn:

```text
Reality
↓
Truth
↓
Fundamentals
↓
First Principles
↓
New Possibilities
```

because every major breakthrough begins by questioning assumptions.
