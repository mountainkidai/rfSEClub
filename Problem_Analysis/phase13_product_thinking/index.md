> A solution is useless if nobody uses it.

---

Most engineers fall in love with technology.

Users fall in love with outcomes.

---

Engineers often think:

```text
Look How Advanced
My Technology Is
```

Users think:

```text
Can You Solve My Problem?
```

---

Nobody buys a drill because they want a drill.

They buy it because they want:

```text
A Hole
```

---

Nobody buys Sari because it uses AI.

They buy it because they want:

```text
Better Health
```

---

Nobody uses Phunsuk because it has software.

They use it because they want:

```text
Better Travel Experiences
```

---

Technology is not the product.

The outcome is the product.
