# PART 1 — WHY MODELS EXIST

---

## 14.1 Reality Is Too Complex

The real world contains:

- Billions of variables
- Incomplete information
- Noise
- Uncertainty

Humans cannot reason about raw reality directly.

## 14.2 What Is A Model?

Definition:


Examples:

```text

=
Model of Building

Neural Network
```
