# PHASE 3 — FUNCTIONS

## The Mathematics of Systems, Change, and Prediction

---

# Goal

Arithmetic teaches:

```text
Quantity
```

Algebra teaches:

```text
Relationships
```

Functions teach:

```text
Systems
```

At the end of this phase, students should understand:

```text
Variables
↓
Relationships
↓
Functions
↓
Systems
↓
Models
↓
Predictions
```

And be able to answer:

> How do we mathematically describe a system?

> How do we predict outputs from inputs?

> How do software systems, physical systems, businesses, and AI models transform inputs into outputs?
