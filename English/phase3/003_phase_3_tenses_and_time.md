# PART 2 — SIMPLE TENSES

The foundation of all tenses.

---

## 3.7 Simple Present

### Structure

```text
Subject + Base Verb
```

Examples:

```text
I study English.
She works remotely.
```

Uses:

- Habits
- Facts
- General truths

---

## 3.8 Simple Past

### Structure

```text
Subject + Past Verb
```

Examples:

```text
I studied English.
She worked remotely.
```

Uses:

- Completed actions
- Historical events

---

## 3.9 Simple Future

### Structure

```text
Subject + will + Verb
```

Examples:

```text
I will study English.
She will work remotely.
```

Uses:

- Predictions
- Future plans
- Promises

---

## 3.10 Comparing Simple Tenses

```text
I work.
I worked.
I will work.
```

## 3.13 Past Continuous

## 3.14 Future Continuous

# PART 4 — PERFECT TENSES

Actions connected across time.

---

## 3.17 What Is Perfect Aspect?

Understanding completion.

---

## 3.18 Present Perfect

### Structure

```text
Subject + has/have + Past Participle
```

Examples:

```text
I have finished.
She has completed the task.
```

Uses:

- Life experiences
- Completed actions affecting now

---

## 3.19 Past Perfect

### Structure

```text
Subject + had + Past Participle
```

Examples:

```text
I had finished before he arrived.
```

---

## 3.20 Future Perfect

### Structure

```text
Subject + will have + Past Participle
```

Examples:

```text
I will have finished by tomorrow.
```

---

## 3.21 Perfect Tense Timelines
