# PHASE 4 — VOCABULARY

## Building the Building Blocks of Thought

---

# Goal

At the end of this phase, you should understand:

```text
Words
↓
Meanings
↓
Relationships
↓
Vocabulary
↓
Expression
↓
Thinking
```

And be able to answer:

> How do humans build and expand vocabulary, and why does vocabulary directly affect thinking, communication, and intelligence?

This phase focuses on one of the most important truths about language:

```text
The size of your vocabulary
determines the precision of your thinking.
```

A person with 500 words thinks differently from a person with 50,000 words.
