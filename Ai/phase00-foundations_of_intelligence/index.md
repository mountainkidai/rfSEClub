# Phase 0 — Foundations of Intelligence

## Introduction

Artificial Intelligence is not primarily about machines.

It is about intelligence.

Before studying neural networks, machine learning, transformers, or agents, we must understand the deeper questions:

```text
What is intelligence?

What is knowledge?

What is learning?

What is information?

What is reasoning?

What is prediction?

Can intelligence emerge from simple rules?

Can intelligence be engineered?
```

The purpose of this phase is to construct a first-principles understanding of intelligence itself.

Everything in the remaining AI curriculum builds on these foundations.
