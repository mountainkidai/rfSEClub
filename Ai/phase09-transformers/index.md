# Phase 9 — Transformers

## Introduction

Language is a sequence.

Traditional language models processed sequences one token at a time.

```text
Word 1
↓
Word 2
↓
Word 3
↓
Word 4
```

This created major problems:

```text
Slow Training

Poor Long-Term Memory

Difficult Parallelization

Information Bottlenecks
```

Transformers introduced a revolutionary idea:

```text
Every Token
Can Directly Look At
Every Other Token
```

This mechanism is called:

```text
Attention
```

Attention became the foundation of modern AI.

The central insight of this phase is:

```text
Intelligence
=
Information Routing
```

Transformers are information routing machines.
