# PHASE 2 — PARTS OF SPEECH

## Understanding the Roles Words Play

---

# Goal

At the end of this phase, you should understand:

```text
Words
↓
Functions
↓
Sentences
↓
Meaning
```

And be able to answer:

> Why does every word in a sentence exist, and what role is it performing?

This phase teaches the grammar engine of English.

Before learning tenses, writing, speaking, or persuasion, students must understand:

```text
Every word has a job.
```

Parts of Speech are those jobs.
