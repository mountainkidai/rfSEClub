# PHASE 6 — WRITING

## Turning Thoughts Into Clear Communication

---

# Goal

At the end of this phase, you should understand:

```text
Thoughts
↓
Ideas
↓
Words
↓
Sentences
↓
Paragraphs
↓
Documents
↓
Communication
```

And be able to answer:

> How do humans transform ideas into clear, useful, and persuasive writing?

Writing is not:

```text
Putting words on paper
```

Writing is:

```text
Thinking
↓
Structuring
↓
Communicating
```

This phase teaches students how to express knowledge, ideas, arguments, and solutions through writing.
