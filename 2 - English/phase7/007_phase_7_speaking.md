# PHASE 7 — SPEAKING

## Turning Thoughts Into Speech

---

# Goal

At the end of this phase, you should understand:

```text
Thought
↓
Language
↓
Speech
↓
Communication
↓
Influence
↓
Leadership
```

And be able to answer:

> How do humans communicate ideas effectively through speech?

Speaking is not:

```text
Talking
```

Speaking is:

```text
Thinking
↓
Structuring
↓
Communicating
↓
Influencing
```

This phase teaches students how to communicate clearly, confidently, and effectively in conversations, interviews, meetings, presentations, and leadership situations.
