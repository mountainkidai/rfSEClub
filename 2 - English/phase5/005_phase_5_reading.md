# PHASE 5 — READING

## Understanding Ideas Written by Others

---

# Goal

At the end of this phase, you should understand:

```text
Symbols
↓
Words
↓
Sentences
↓
Paragraphs
↓
Documents
↓
Knowledge
↓
Understanding
```

And be able to answer:

> How do humans extract knowledge from written language?

Reading is not:

```text
Looking at words
```

Reading is:

```text
Converting symbols
↓
into meaning
↓
into knowledge
↓
into understanding
```

This phase teaches students how to learn from books, articles, documentation, research papers, and technical specifications.
