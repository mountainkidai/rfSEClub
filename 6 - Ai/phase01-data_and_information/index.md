Phase 1 is where AI stops being philosophy and becomes engineering.

Phase 0 answered:

```text
What is intelligence?

What is learning?

What is prediction?

What is reasoning?
```

Now we ask:

```text
What is information?

How is information represented?

How do machines store information?

How do machines extract meaning from information?
```

This phase is more important than Neural Networks.

More important than Deep Learning.

More important than Transformers.

Because every AI system is ultimately:

```text
Information
↓
Representation
↓
Learning
↓
Prediction
```

If the representation is bad:

```text
No model can save you.
```

# Phase 1 — Data & Information

## Introduction

Artificial Intelligence begins with information.

Without information:

```text
No learning

No prediction

No reasoning

No intelligence
```

Everything an AI system does originates from data.

The central question of this phase is:

```text
How does reality become information?
```

The entire AI field is built on the transformation:

```text
Reality
↓
Observation
↓
Data
↓
Information
↓
Representation
↓
Knowledge
```

Understanding this transformation is essential.
