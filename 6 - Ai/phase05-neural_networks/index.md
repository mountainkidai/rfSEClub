# Phase 5 — Neural Networks

## Introduction

For decades AI systems depended heavily on human-designed representations.

Engineers manually decided:

```text
What matters?

What should be measured?

What features should exist?
```

Neural networks introduced a radically different idea:

```text
Let the machine learn the representation.
```

Instead of programming intelligence:

```text
Rules
↓
Predictions
```

we create a system capable of discovering its own internal rules.

Neural networks are computational systems inspired by biological learning.

They are not brains.

But they borrow one critical idea:

```text
Large numbers of simple units
↓
Connected together
↓
Can produce complex behavior
```

This phase teaches neural networks from first principles.
