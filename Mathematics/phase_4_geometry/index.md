# PART 1 — THE INVENTION OF GEOMETRY

Understanding why geometry exists.

---

## 4.1 Life Before Geometry

### Central Question

How did humans measure the world before geometry existed?

### Historical Problems

Ancient civilizations needed to answer:

- How much land do I own?
- Where does my property end?
- How tall is a pyramid?
- How far away is the river?
- How much stone is needed for construction?

### Case Study

Every year the Nile River flooded.

Property boundaries disappeared.

Egyptians needed a way to reconstruct land ownership.

This problem helped create geometry.
